Project Domain: Education

Limited Interdisciplinary Learning Opportunities

Despite the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020 promoting a flexible and multidisciplinary education model, most Indian higher education institutions still function in rigid disciplinary silos.

Understanding the Issues

Why This Happens

  • Rigid curriculum frameworks and outdated regulations
  • Lack of collaboration between departments
  • Shortage of faculty trained in interdisciplinary methods
  • Institutional resistance to structural reforms

Why It Matters

Without interdisciplinary learning, students miss out on creative problem-solving skills, holistic thinking, and innovation opportunities—core elements in the NEP 2020 vision.
